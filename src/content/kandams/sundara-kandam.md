intro: >
  The Sundara Kandam ("the beautiful book") follows Hanuman as he leaps the ocean
  to Lanka in search of Sita. It holds some of the epic's most beloved episodes —
  the crossing of the sea, the search through Lanka, the meeting with Sita in the
  Ashoka grove and the giving of the choodamani, the confrontation in Ravana's
  court, the burning of Lanka, and the return with Sita's message to Rama. In the
  Sri Vaishnava tradition it is cherished as the book of Hanuman's flawless
  service (kainkaryam) and of Sita's steadfast devotion.
