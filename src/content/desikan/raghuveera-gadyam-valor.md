Swami Vedanta Desika's Raghuveera Gadyam glorifies Rama precisely as the heroic destroyer
of the rakshasa forces — the theme Kamban's Pāyiram v.12 names in "தோள் வலி கூறுவார்க்கே"
(for those who sing of Rama's arm-strength).
